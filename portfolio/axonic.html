Anna Ronco Graphic and UX Designer

Axonic Systems, a Swiss startup, develops soft- and hardware solutions for fitness centers. It aims to enable full automatic tracking for training exercises. I was asked to create a simple visual identification and give it a feel of smart and innovative high-tech solutions. The logo idea is based on a progress bar, which represents training and statistics. Minimal, sharp form are to enhance the perception of Axonic as a modern, precise and trastworthy brand.
