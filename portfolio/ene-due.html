Ene Due

This illustration story is my contribution to the book created by students of Warsaw Art Academy and dedicated to young readers. Ene Due is a children's word game. Using the words themselves for a typographic illustration I constructed the setting of the story. To make the overall impression appealing to young readers I chose to hand-draw cartoon characters and color them with an energetic orange.
