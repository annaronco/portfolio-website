ABOUT

My name is Anna Ronco and I'm a graphic and UX designer based in Zurich.

I deal with branding and enjoy co-creating new brands with their logo, a visual identity and a unique story. I'm interested in illustration and typography and explore these areas working on print - books, brochures, posters, fliers, T-shirts, as well as creating web and social media content. I have experience with large web portals, conducting user experience research, wireframing, designing, documenting UIs and usability testing. Recently, I'm also making my first steps in front-end web development.

Please contact me if you like my work and think that I could be a good partner for your project!
