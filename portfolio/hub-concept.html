Anna Ronco Graphic and UX Designer

HUB.concept is a creative studio based in Fribourg, Switzerland. I was invited to join the small team and together conceptualize and present our working process and the service offer. My main focus in creating the logo, the branding concept, and the website was to reflect the company's positive and personal attitude towards building new relationships with customers. We strived to make the access to design services quick and easy, but at the same time give the maximum personal attention to the customer's needs.
